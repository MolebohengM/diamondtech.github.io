About Us


Diamond Tech was established in 2015 by Fortune Makoni. As a company we pride ourselves in doing an amazing job when it comes to prioritizing our clients needs. We believe that there is no us without our clients. Our aim is to continue building a strong relationship with our clients. Our business modal is to partner with global ICT brands and expand our company and expertise.

Our company director, Fortune Makoni has more than 13 years of experience in the ICT field. He obtained his qualifitions from STEM Academy where during his years of study, participated in hackathons that were ogranized by the MICT Department of the academy. Since then he has never looked back.
